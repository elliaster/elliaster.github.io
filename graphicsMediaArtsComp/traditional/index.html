Traditional Art Traditional Art is art made using paper, canvas or other physical surfaces with art supplies such as: pencils, brushes, paints, etc. These traditional arts are continued by many artists as it is easily made any where at anytime and can produce great results. People still use traditional mediums such as paint because they offer a satisfactory feeling or greater look than that of a digital one. There is a texture dependant on what medium you use, and that can be replecated by computers but to a lesser extent.
