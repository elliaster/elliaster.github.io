Impact Digital Illustration has done a huge deal of good to the world. It makes art accessible to anyone in any age group who has access to any digital device. This allows you the tools to let your imagination run wild and come up with new worlds or the like. As technology progresses Digital Illustration has evolved even further with emerging technologies like Virtual and Augmented Reality. Digital Illustration has even broken into the third dimension just as sculptures had centuries before. The use of Virtual Reality allows people to sculpt or drawn in three dimensions, and many people have created simply amazing pieces of art.
