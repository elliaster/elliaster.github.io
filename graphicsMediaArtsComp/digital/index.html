Digital Art Digital Art is in layman terms art that is created on a computer or any other digital device. This can take the form of a piece of work on a digital canvas or the like. Digital Art has no defined metric to compare it to as it is limitless, only bound by the imagination of the artist. If you can imagine it, and you have the skills / knowhow then you are set to create your vision.
