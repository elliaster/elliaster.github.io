The Babadook

Release Date : January 17, 2014

Genre : Drama, Horror

Director : Jennifer Kent

Rating : Not Rated

Run Time : 1 Hour 33 Mins

Why I like it :

I like this movie because my favorite genre is horror, and one of my favorite sub-genres of horror is psychological. The main character of this is a mom that is going through a lot of emotional distress and metal instability after losing her husband in a car accident, going eventually to the extreme to get rid of 'The Babadook'

Synopsis :

A widow, and her son are tormented by a 'evil spirit' called the Babadook, after reading the book of the same name. The mother is trying to cope with the problems she starts facing after the Babadook starts appearing in her everyday life, along with her son's fears of monsters everywhere. The Babadook stalks her in her everyday life.

Reason for Colour Scheme :

I used the colour scheme based off the promotional posters that is shown on the left. The dark red colour of the Title and sub-titles comes from the title on the poster. The almost black colour is from the babadook's dark figure and the gray is from the the grayscale actors in the window of the doorway or window in the babadook's sillouette.
