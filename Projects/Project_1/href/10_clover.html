10 Cloverfield Lane

Release Date : March 11, 2016

Genre : Drama, Horror, Mystery

Director : Ridley Scott

Rating : PG-13

Run Time : 1 Hour 44 Mins

Why I like it :

I like the movie because the creepy and mysterious person that is Howard, and what is going on. The setting also is executed really well. The movie it'self takes place in single location, and still somehow finds a way to keep everything that is happening in the movie interesting. Especially the tense moments in the movie.

Synopsis :

A woman named Michelle, who got into an accident wakes up in a underground shelter with two different men. Howard and Emmett are the two men that live in the shelter. Howard explains to Michelle that there is no way she can leave because of the nuclear fallout that had poisoned the air around the area. After seeing Howard's red truck she remembers that she was hit off the road by Harold who was trying as quick as possible to get to his shelter. As time goes on they begin to develop a relationship similar to that of a family, where Michelle is treated as a little girl by Harold, and Howard is intolerant with Emmett. And then things begin to change.
