Kubo and the Two Strings

Release Date : August 19, 2016

Genre : Animation, Adventure, Family

Director : Travis Knight

Rating : PG

Run Time : 1 Hour 41 Mins

Why I like it :

The reason I like this film is because of the artistic style it has. The whole movie is shot in stop motion, which is one of my favorite animation styles because when it's executed well it is really apparent. This movie shows a high level of professionalism. You can tell that they spent time and dedication to this movie to make it as good as possible. I also like the story because of the journey the main character Kubo goes on with friends he makes along the way to solve the mystery of his dead father.

Synopsis :

A kid name Kubo who has never the outside world from his small town where he puts on shows for the towns people using oragami puppets he manipulates with his shamisen. He tells the story of his father, but can not tell the ending because he himself doesn't know how it ends, and his mother can't tell him because of her deteriorating mind. Kubo has to leave to find out what happened to his father.
