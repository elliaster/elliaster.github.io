Business Cards

These business cards are the next step to the project. In this project I have to incorporate the logos into 3 business cards per each logo, ending up with 9 different business cards of varying styles. The cards are supposed to show a name, email address, phone number, and address of the business, basically all the necessary contact information for a business.
